Course Overview

Level: 3rd semesterCredits: 2 (1 Theory + 1 Practical)Duration: 15 Weeks

This course explores organ-system development from the end of gastrulation to the fetal period, emphasizing molecular regulation, morphogenesis, and clinical correlation as presented in Langman’s Medical Embryology (15th ed., 2024). Students integrate embryology with anatomy, histology, pathology, and imaging to interpret normal and abnormal development.

Course Objectives

  • Describe the sequential development of major organ systems derived from ectoderm, mesoderm, and endoderm.
  • Explain key signaling pathways that regulate organogenesis (e.g., SHH, FGF, BMP, WNT, HOX gene families).
  • Correlate normal development with the embryological basis of congenital anomalies.
  • Apply embryological principles to interpret clinical images and genetic disorders.
  • Integrate embryological knowledge into case-based reasoning and early clinical sciences.

2Musculoskeletal SystemSomite derivatives; intramembranous vs. endochondral ossification; limb bud patterning (HOX, FGF10, SHH); anomalies (amelia, phocomelia, clubfoot).

4Cardiovascular II — Vessels & Fetal ↔ Postnatal CirculationAortic arches, venous and lymphatic development; fetal shunts and closures; PDA, coarctation, double arch.
5Respiratory SystemLaryngotracheal diverticulum; branching morphogenesis; alveolar maturation; regulators (NKX2-1, FGF/BMP); TEF, hypoplasia.
6Digestive I — Gut Tube & MesenteriesForegut, midgut, hindgut; rotation & fixation; Meckel’s diverticulum, malrotation, atresias.
7Digestive II — Liver, GB, Pancreas, SpleenHepatic diverticulum; pancreatic buds; molecular control (FGF, BMP, PDX1); annular pancreas, biliary atresia.
8Urogenital I — UrinaryPronephros → mesonephros → metanephros; ureteric bud–metanephric blastema; renal agenesis, horseshoe kidney, duplicated ureter.
9Urogenital II — ReproductiveGonadal differentiation; ductal system; external genitalia; DSDs incl. hypospadias and cryptorchidism.
10Head & NeckPharyngeal arches/pouches/clefts; face, palate, tongue, teeth; neural crest migration; FGF8, BMP4, SHH; cleft lip/palate, Treacher Collins, DiGeorge.
11Central Nervous SystemNeurulation; primary/secondary brain vesicles; spinal cord; PAX/OTX2/SHH roles; NTDs (anencephaly, spina bifida).
12Peripheral NS & AutonomicNeural crest & placodes; cranial/spinal nerves; autonomic development; Hirschsprung disease.
13Special Senses — Eye, Ear, NoseOptic cup & lens (PAX6); otic placode & membranous labyrinth; nasal placode to choanae; coloboma, microtia, choanal atresia.
14Endocrine & Placenta + Integrated ReviewPituitary, thyroid, parathyroid, adrenal, pancreatic islets; placental development and barrier; integrated clinical cases & teratology.
